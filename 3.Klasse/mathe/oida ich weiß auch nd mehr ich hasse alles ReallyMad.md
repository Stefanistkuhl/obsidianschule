### produkt regel 
$$f'(x)=u'*v+u*v'$$
```math
||{"id":463126828029}||

f(x)=x³*x⁵
u=x³
v=x^5
u'=3x²
v=5x⁴
f'(x)=3x²*x⁵+x³+5x⁴=3x⁷+5x⁷=8x⁷
```

### quatientenregel 
$$f(x)=\frac{u'*v-u*v'}{v²}$$

||{"id":139569537811}||

f(x)=\frac{2x+3}{x⁵}
u=2x+3=2*1+0=2
v=x⁵=5*x⁴
f'(x)=\frac{2*x⁵-(2x+3)*5x⁴}{(x⁵)²}
